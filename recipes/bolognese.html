Bolognese

Description

Bolognese is a warm and rich meat sauce fit for the best pasta in the world.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Red wine
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato paste
  • Onions
  • Carrot
  • Garlic
  • Thyme
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Brown beef in olive oil
  2. Dice onions and carrots and saute
  3. Cook in garlic and tomato paste
  4. Deglaze with red wine and reduce slightly
  5. Add in crushed tomatoes and herbs
  6. Season as you go through each step
  7. Cook uncovered in a 275 F oven for 3-4 hours
